Meet Your Health Coach!

My name is Michelle and I have always been on a quest to eat healthier. To eat in a way that sustains our planet, is less cruel to animals and aligns with what our ancestors ate.

My health and wellness path began when I was presented with the option of accepting a buyout to resign from my corporate position of fourteen years. Instead of viewing this as a negative event, I chose to think of it as my chance to change my life. This was my time to do something different; something meaningful that I was passionate about. I had always felt that I was meant to do something greater, so I took the buyout and left my job! I embarked on an incredible journey to heal myself - mind, body and spirit.

I am a kind, energetic, passionate, opinionated and organized person who loves to cook, hike, practice yoga, kayak, snowshoe and swim! I love riding rollercoasters and will ride all the rollercoasters in a theme park. I love being by the water and experiencing its tranquility, but also enjoying all the activities associated with the water. I love being in nature; interacting with my natural environment via all my senses.

I am so glad that I finally chose this career path. I know that I am where I'm supposed to be. I am confident that I can help you navigate your health and wellness hurdles! Together we can create a plan that will bring your goals to actuality.

My Mission

Not only did I want to heal myself, but I wanted to help others realize their true potential. I want others to realize that they are the masters of their fate and we can all do whatever we want to do! To further bring my dream to fruition, I enrolled in the Institute for Integrative Nutrition (IIN) and I am now an Integrative Nutrition Health Coach! I haven't looked back!

It has always been my dream to somehow incorporate the things I love to do into a career. For the longest time, I didn't think that was possible. I now know that it is possible....I get to do all the activities I love and help others by being an Integrative Nutrition Health Coach!!

I have learned so much on this journey and I know that I can impart what I've learned to you. You too can change your trajectory. You can change your story and I can help you figure out how to make that happen. Are you ready to find your new path?
